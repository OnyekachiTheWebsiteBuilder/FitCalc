Hi, I'm Onyekachi 👋

I'm the creator of FitCalc. I built this website because I wanted fitness calculators that were simple, modern, and based on real science. Too many fitness websites are filled with ads, confusing designs, and inaccurate calculators. My goal was to build something that anyone could use in seconds.

FitCalc is still growing. Version 1 includes calorie, protein, and macro calculators, but I'm constantly working on adding more tools, improving accuracy, and creating a better experience for everyone.

Thank you for visiting FitCalc and supporting my project!
