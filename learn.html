How FitCalc Works

Every calculator on FitCalc is built using trusted, science-based formulas used by nutrition and fitness professionals.

🔥 Calorie Calculator

Our calorie calculator estimates your daily calorie needs using the Institute of Medicine (IOM) Estimated Energy Requirement (EER) equations.

It considers:

  • Age
  • Sex
  • Height
  • Weight
  • Activity Level
  • Goal (Lose, Maintain, Gain)

💪 Protein Calculator

Protein recommendations are based on current sports nutrition research.

Your recommendation changes depending on your goal and activity level.

🥗 Macro Calculator

The Macro Calculator divides your daily calories into protein, carbohydrates, and fats using balanced macronutrient ratios.

📚 Scientific References

  • Institute of Medicine (IOM)
  • American College of Sports Medicine (ACSM)
  • International Society of Sports Nutrition (ISSN)
  • USDA FoodData Central
